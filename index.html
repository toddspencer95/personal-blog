Howdy! Welcome to my site, I'm Todd Spencer.

A Mechanical Engineer by education, a Systems Engineer by profession, and a fervent Software Engineer in my spare time. I find immense joy in the art of creation, reveling in the intricate dance between design, functionality, and implementation that engineering offers. My journey has led me through the realms of 3D modeling, coding, data analysis, and Artificial Intelligence, where I seamlessly blend my education with hands-on projects.

Outside the digital canvas, I'm an avid explorer. From my goal to visit every national park to diving into the realms of photography and cinematography, I relish documenting my travels and projects. While fitness has been a constant companion since high school, with a focus on powerlifting and recent ventures into cardio, I also find solace in strumming the guitar and, perhaps fueled by a slight addiction, indulging in a bit too much coffee.

What fuels my endeavors is an insatiable curiosity. Every project begins with a "what if?" prompting a cascade of questions, leading me on a journey of continuous exploration and experimentation. This innate curiosity, perhaps inherited from a long line of engineers and tradespeople in my family, has been a guiding force. Growing up surrounded by the spirit of building and shaping the world, coupled with my inquisitive nature and love of sharing, has shaped the man I am today.

Join me on my voyage, where engineering meets creativity, and curiosity propels us forward.
